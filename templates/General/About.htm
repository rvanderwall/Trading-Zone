Thank you for visiting caracal.

This site allows you to do several things:

  • You can view things for sale.
  • You can register and get additional functionality when you log in such as:
    • You can announce an item for sale.
    • You can unleash a caracal hunter. This hunter will sit and wait for another seller to post an item that you are interested in. For example, if you want a new audio mixer for your jockey job, simply create a hunter with the keyword mixer. Next time someone posts a mixer for sale, say a nice Tascam, you'll get email notification as soon as the posting hits the site.
